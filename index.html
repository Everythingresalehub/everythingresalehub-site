Condition Grade Rank

Fast mapping: A = Like New • B = Very Good • C = Good • D = Fair

  • A (Like New) Minimal or no signs of use. Fully functional. All accessories unless noted.
  • B (Very Good) Light cosmetic wear (small scuffs). Fully functional. Missing minor accessories possible.
  • C (Good) Noticeable wear or cosmetic marks. Fully functional or minor non‑critical issues disclosed.
  • D (Fair) Heavy wear or functional quirks. Still usable; issues clearly described and priced accordingly.
